How It Works

From raw market noise to structured emotional intelligence.

Sentient Signals follows a four-layer workflow: ingest, interpret, score, and guide.

Step 01

Ingest

Collect price action, order flow context, news, social signals, volatility, wallet flows, and macro pressure.

Step 02

Interpret

Classify emotional tone, crowd behavior, narrative strength, fear/greed intensity, and risk regime.

Step 03

Score

Generate signal scores for bullish pressure, bearish pressure, volatility risk, narrative heat, and reversal danger.

Step 04

Guide

Provide SOVRA-ready summaries, alerts, dashboards, scenario planning, and decision-support intelligence.
